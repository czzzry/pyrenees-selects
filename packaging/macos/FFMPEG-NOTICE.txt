Pyrenees Selects invokes separate FFmpeg and FFprobe executables for media
inspection and disposable proxy generation.

The locally built Intel application includes the pinned FFmpeg 8.1.2 binaries
linked from FFmpeg's official download page and produced by evermeet.cx. The
build script verifies exact SHA-256 checksums before packaging. The executable
reports its applicable license when run with `ffmpeg -L`. Source, build and
licensing information are available from:

https://ffmpeg.org/
https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html
https://evermeet.cx/ffmpeg/

The generated .app is intended for personal use on the Mac that built it. Do
not publish the generated application bundle without reviewing the license of
the exact FFmpeg build included and supplying all corresponding obligations.
